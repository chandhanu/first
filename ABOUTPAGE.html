The PSG Sons and Charities

The trust started in 1926, has worked towards building on ideals of Gandhism and in the process educated over 300,000 students mostly from economically weaker sections of the society. As India moves into the global stage with the world’s largest youth population and ethics, PSG’s ideal of creating high capacity matched with stellar quality would find greater resonance with all those who serve for the common good of the society. PSG Institutions are unique where growth, excellence, equality and ethics are considered together to built future generation and contribute to the nation.

PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research (PSG iTech), Coimbatore

PSG iTech, the latest initiative of PSG and Sons’ Charities, aims to realize its objective of enhancing youth empowerment through technical education. This institute caters to various engineering disciplines, focusing on learning, industry engagement of students, innovative and inclusive pedagogy, and ethics. PSG Institute of Technology and Applied Research aims at effective transfer of knowledge, pursuit of truth and moulding the students to become ideal citizens of the country.

Sree Saraswathi Thyagaraja College (STC), Pollachi (An Autonomous Institution)

STC was established in 1997, the Golden Jubilee year of India’s independence, by V.S.V. Vidhyaa Mandir, a charitable trust. The trust, recognizing the need for good quality general and professional higher education in rural areas, set-up the college with a determined vision to provide such education to students from rural areas in and around Pollachi.

The College is affiliated to Bharathiar University and the MBA and MCA programmes have been approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. Set in sylvan surroundings, the College ensures that students learn well in a conducive academic environment with the state-of-the-art infrastructure facilities. The College has added another feather to its cap by becoming the first Institution in Pollachi Educational District to obtain the coveted ISO 9001:2000 Certification from the International Standards Organization through National Quality Assurance Registrar, United Kingdom.
